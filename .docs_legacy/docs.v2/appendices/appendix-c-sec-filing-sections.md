# Appendix C: SEC Filing Section Reference

> Referenced from [System Specification](../system_specification.md)

---

## 10-K Standard Sections (as of 2024)

### Part I

| Section Key | Title | Importance | Content |
|-------------|-------|------------|---------|
| `item_1` | Business | **High** | Company overview, products/services, customers, competition, regulation, employees, organizational structure |
| `item_1a` | Risk Factors | **Critical** | Material risks to the business, market, financial condition. Often 20–50 pages. Key for qualitative analysis. |
| `item_1b` | Unresolved Staff Comments | Low | Outstanding SEC staff review comments |
| `item_1c` | Cybersecurity | Medium | Cybersecurity risk management, strategy, governance. New requirement starting 2023. |
| `item_2` | Properties | Low | Description of material physical properties |
| `item_3` | Legal Proceedings | Medium | Material litigation, regulatory actions |
| `item_4` | Mine Safety Disclosures | Low | Only applicable to mining companies |

### Part II

| Section Key | Title | Importance | Content |
|-------------|-------|------------|---------|
| `item_5` | Market for Registrant's Common Equity | Medium | Stock exchange, dividends, share repurchases, stock performance comparison |
| `item_6` | Reserved | None | Previously "Selected Financial Data" — eliminated by SEC in 2021 |
| `item_7` | Management's Discussion and Analysis (MD&A) | **Critical** | Management's perspective on financial results, liquidity, capital resources, outlook. Often 30–60 pages. **THE most important section for qualitative analysis.** |
| `item_7a` | Quantitative and Qualitative Disclosures About Market Risk | Medium | Interest rate risk, foreign currency risk, commodity risk |
| `item_8` | Financial Statements and Supplementary Data | **Critical** | Full financial statements (IS, BS, CF, equity statement) plus notes. Typically 40–80 pages. Primary source for quantitative analysis. |
| `item_9` | Changes in and Disagreements with Accountants | Low | — |
| `item_9a` | Controls and Procedures | Medium | Internal controls assessment, auditor opinion on controls |

### Part III

| Section Key | Title | Importance |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| `item_10` | Directors, Executive Officers and Corporate Governance | Low |
| `item_11` | Executive Compensation | Medium |
| `item_12` | Security Ownership of Certain Beneficial Owners | Low |
| `item_13` | Certain Relationships and Related Transactions | Low |
| `item_14` | Principal Accountant Fees and Services | Low |
